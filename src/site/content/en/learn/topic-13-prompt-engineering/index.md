## Why this topic

Prompt engineering is not just technical skill — it's communication. The ability to explain *why* a prompt is designed a certain way, to defend your choices in a review, and to teach others the patterns is what separates a Principal Engineer from a developer who just writes prompts that work sometimes.

## Structure

This topic has 4 modules:

1. **Content Outline** — Core concepts sorted by importance
2. **Key Vocabulary** — Phrases and words you must internalize
3. **Practice Questions** — 5 questions to drill until automatic
4. **Weekly Goals** — Specific targets for Week 1 and Week 2

Use the sidebar navigation to move through each module.

## KPI 2 connection

Each lesson maps to a section of your LLM Best Practices Guide. As you complete each lesson, add the key concepts to the relevant section of your guide.
