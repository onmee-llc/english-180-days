## Week 1 Goal

Explain chain-of-thought, few-shot, and structured output prompting — each in under 90 seconds, with a real example from your work — without notes.

**How to verify:** Record yourself explaining all three. If you can do it in 4 minutes total with no hesitation, Week 1 is done.

## Week 2 Goal

Answer all 5 practice questions without preparation. For Question 5 (Best Practices Guide), deliver a 3-minute overview that covers all sections of your KPI 2 guide.

**How to verify:** Do a full dry run — start the recording, ask yourself the question, answer as if it's a real review with your tech lead.

## KPI 2 action

This topic maps directly to your KPI 2 guide. At the end of each lesson, write one bullet point in English for the relevant section of your team's LLM Best Practices Guide. By Day 168, your draft should be 80% complete.

## If you need more time

If the goals don't feel comfortable after 2 weeks, extend by 3–5 days before moving on.
**Fluency over schedule.**
