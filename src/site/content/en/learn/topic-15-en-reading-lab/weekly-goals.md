## Week 1 (Days 183–189): Establish the habit

**Reading target:** 7 articles (1 per day). Alternate: ByteByteGo → Anthropic → ByteByteGo →…

**Skills focus:**
- Follow the 3-part Reading Lab method every session: Skim → Vocabulary → Summary
- Aim for summaries under 30 words
- Write vocabulary in English only — no Vietnamese definitions

**Weekly checkpoint:**
- [ ] Completed 7 reading sessions (Days 183–189)
- [ ] Written 7 one-sentence summaries
- [ ] Extracted ≥40 vocabulary terms total (minimum 5–8 per session)
- [ ] Summary quality improving — earlier ones are longer and rougher, later ones more precise

## Week 2 (Days 190–196): Build speed and apply vocabulary

**Reading target:** 7 articles (1 per day).

**Skills focus:**
- Reduce skim time to under 5 minutes for a 1500-word article
- Start using Reading Lab vocabulary in your daily KPI 1 or 2 work writing
- Day 196 (Lesson 14): choose your own article independently — no prompt needed

**Weekly checkpoint:**
- [ ] Completed 7 reading sessions (Days 190–196)
- [ ] Day 196 article was chosen and processed independently (no lesson prompt)
- [ ] Used at least 5 Reading Lab vocabulary terms in real writing or in a meeting this week
- [ ] Reading log contains 14 entries

## Topic 15 Completion Checklist

- [ ] 14 reading sessions completed (Days 183–196)
- [ ] 14 one-sentence summaries written in English
- [ ] 70–100 vocabulary terms extracted and logged
- [ ] Can apply the Reading Lab method to any new article without prompts
- [ ] KPI 5 log shows 14 articles as part of the 50+ target for 2026
