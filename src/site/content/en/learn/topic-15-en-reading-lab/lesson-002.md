## Part 1 — Skim (5 minutes)

Research articles have a different structure from engineering blog posts:
- **Abstract** — the full argument in 2–4 sentences
- **Introduction** — the problem and prior work
- **Method** — how the approach works
- **Results** — what the experiments showed
- **Discussion / Limitations** — what doesn't work yet

Read the abstract, section headings, and the first sentence of each section. Then close it and answer:
> "What problem does this research address, and what is the proposed solution?"

|------|-----------|
