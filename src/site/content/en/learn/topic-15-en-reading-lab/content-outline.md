## Skills progression

**Lessons 1–4:** Establish the Reading Lab habit. Focus on recognizing article structure and extracting defined technical terms.

**Lessons 5–8:** Build speed. Skim in under 5 minutes. Prioritize vocabulary from your own working area (AI system design and safety).

**Lessons 9–12:** Improve summary quality. The one-sentence summary should be clear enough that a non-specialist can understand it.

**Lessons 13–14:** Self-directed. Choose your own article from the approved sources. Apply the full method without the lesson prompting you.

## Learning objectives

By the end of Topic 15, you will be able to:
- Read a 1500-word technical article in English without a dictionary within 15 minutes
- Extract 5–8 key technical terms from any article and use them in writing or speech
- Summarize the main argument of any article in one English sentence under 30 words
- Maintain a reading log of 14 completed articles from ByteByteGo and Anthropic Research
