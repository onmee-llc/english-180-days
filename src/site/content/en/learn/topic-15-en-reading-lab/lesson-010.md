**Starter vocabulary:**

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| jailbreak | an adversarial prompt designed to bypass a model's safety behaviors |
| prompt injection | an attack where malicious content in the environment hijacks the model's instructions |
| content moderation | the process of detecting and filtering harmful or policy-violating content |
| guardrail | a constraint that limits what a model will produce or how it will behave |
| safe messaging | communication guidelines for sensitive topics (e.g., mental health) that reduce risk of harm |
| dual-use content | information or capability that can serve both beneficial and harmful purposes |
| ASL (AI Safety Level) | Anthropic's internal scale for classifying model capability levels and safety requirements |
| responsible scaling policy | Anthropic's commitment to not deploy models that exceed a given capability threshold without safety mitigations |
