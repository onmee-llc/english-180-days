## Session goal

Complete the first full practice run of Tech Talk #1. Timed. Recorded. No notes.

## Tech Talk #1 structure (15-minute version)

Use this as your practice outline:

**00:00 – 01:00 — Opening hook** (from Day 170)

**01:00 – 02:00 — Problem framing**
> Why is LLM integration harder than it looks? What breaks in production that doesn't break in a notebook?

**02:00 – 06:00 — Technical core (3 points)**
1. Reliability: retry, fallback, circuit breaker
2. Cost: model selection, token logging, caching
3. Observability: what you monitor and why

**06:00 – 09:00 — Live demo narration** (from Day 172)
> Walk through your KPI 1 system — show it working, narrate every step.

**09:00 – 12:00 — Key architecture decisions**
> Why did you choose this provider? Why this data store? What would you change?

**12:00 – 14:00 — What the team should take away**
> The 2–3 patterns they should apply to their next LLM feature.

**14:00 – 15:00 — Closing and call to action**
