## Session goal

Narrate a 3-minute live demo of your KPI 1 system without any silence longer than 2 seconds.

## Shadowing passage

> Narrating a live demo is harder than it looks because you're doing two things simultaneously: operating the software and explaining it. The number one failure mode is silence — the presenter clicks through the interface saying nothing for 10 seconds, leaving the audience to guess what they're supposed to be noticing. My narration rule is this: if I haven't said anything in 3 seconds, I need to say something. Even "let me zoom in on this" or "I'm navigating to the settings page here" is better than silence. Here's my narration framework for a demo. Before I do anything: "Let me show you what this looks like from the user's perspective. I'm going to type a question — the same kind of question our internal engineers ask every day." While I'm doing it: "I've just submitted the query. What's happening right now under the hood is the system is retrieving the top-five relevant document sections — you can see the API call going out here in the network panel." After the result appears: "The answer came back in 3.1 seconds. The key thing to notice is the citations at the bottom — those are the actual document sections the answer is grounded in. If the answer is wrong, you can trace it directly to the source." This framework — before, during, after — gives you the narration skeleton. Fill in the details from your specific system.

## 30-minute protocol

**Minutes 1–5** — Read silently twice.
**Minutes 6–12** — Shadow the passage 3 times.
**Minutes 13–22** — Open your KPI 1 system. Narrate a 3-minute walkthrough using the before/during/after framework.
**Minutes 23–28** — Record the demo narration. Check: any silence longer than 2 seconds?
**Minutes 29–30** — Note the moment you went silent. Prepare a filler phrase for that point.

## Reflection

What is the hardest part of your demo to narrate? Prepare 3 sentences specifically for that moment.

## Anti-Translation Drill

Narrate the first 60 seconds of your demo from memory. English only. No silence longer than 2 seconds.
