## Why this topic

Delivering a technical talk in a second language is one of the hardest public speaking challenges. Most engineers can answer questions one-on-one but fall apart when they need to hold a room for 45 minutes. This topic closes that gap.

You have two real deliverables: Tech Talk #1 in July and Tech Talk #2 in November. Every lesson here is training for those two events.

## Structure

This topic has 4 modules:

1. **Content Outline** — Core concepts sorted by importance
2. **Key Vocabulary** — Phrases and words you must internalize
3. **Practice Questions** — 5 questions to drill until automatic
4. **Weekly Goals** — Specific targets for Week 1 and Week 2

Use the sidebar navigation to move through each module.
