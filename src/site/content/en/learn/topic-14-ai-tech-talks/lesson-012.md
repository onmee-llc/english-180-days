## Session goal

Complete a full outline for Tech Talk #2 and practice the opening and closing.

## Tech Talk #2 outline framework

**Opening hook (1 min)**
> "Six months ago, I presented this same team an architecture for our internal AI system. Today I want to show you what actually happened — including what broke, what we fixed, and what I'd do differently. No polished success story. Just what production taught us."

**Current system state (2 min)**
- Key metrics: latency, error rate, cost, usage volume
- What works well — be specific
- What is still a known limitation

**Three Lessons Learned (9 min — 3 min each)**
- Lesson 1: The technical failure — setup, trigger, impact, resolution
- Lesson 2: The design decision we regret — what the ADR said, what production showed
- Lesson 3: The surprise win — something that worked better than expected and why

**Eval framework — how we know it's working (3 min)**
- Our eval set: what's in it, how we use it
- What LangSmith showed us that logs didn't
- The quality metric we wish we'd had from day one

**AI Roadmap for 2027 (3 min)** (from Day 178)

**Closing and call to action (2 min)**

## 30-minute protocol

**Minutes 1–5** — Read the outline framework.
**Minutes 6–12** — Fill in your specific content for each section.
**Minutes 13–22** — Practice the opening hook and closing aloud. Record both.
**Minutes 23–28** — Review the three Lessons Learned. Can you tell each one in under 3 minutes?
**Minutes 29–30** — Record the Lesson 1 summary. Is the trigger specific?

## Reflection
