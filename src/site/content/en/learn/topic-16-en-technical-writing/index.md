## What this track is about

**KPI 7:** Write and publish one English technical article (Dev.to, Medium, or LinkedIn).
**KPI 6:** Participate in 4 English-language speaking sessions (team presentations, 1:1 discussions, knowledge-sharing sessions).

These two KPIs are tightly connected — the process of writing a technical article is the best preparation for speaking about technical topics in English. Each lesson in this track works on both.

## The article you will write

Your article topic must come from your real work on KPI 1 or KPI 2. This is not a generic tutorial — it's a practitioner's reflection. Choose one:

- **Option A:** "What I Learned Building an Internal LLM System" — practical lessons from KPI 1
- **Option B:** "The LLM Prompt Engineering Mistakes I Made (And Fixed)" — from KPI 2
- **Option C:** "From Architecture Diagram to Production: LLM System Design in Practice" — combining KPI 1 + 2

## Publishing platforms

| Platform | Audience | Best for |
|----------|----------|---------|
| [Dev.to](https://dev.to/) | Developer community | Technical tutorials and practitioner reflections |
| [Medium](https://medium.com/) | Mixed technical and non-technical | Broader reach; may need publication |
| [LinkedIn Articles](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/) | Professional network | Visibility with colleagues and hiring managers |

**Recommendation:** Publish on Dev.to first (developer-native, easy formatting), then cross-post to LinkedIn.

## The writing process

This track follows a structured writing process:

1. **Topic selection** — KPI-aligned topic, narrow scope
2. **Outline in English** — structure before you write
3. **First draft** — write fast, edit later
4. **Grammar + clarity review** — use Claude or ChatGPT for grammar, Hemingway Editor for clarity
5. **Final draft** — one round of human editing
6. **Publishing** — Dev.to + LinkedIn cross-post

## Speaking sessions (KPI 6)

Each week, one lesson focuses on preparing for an English-language speaking session:
- Presenting the article draft to a colleague
- Leading a 15-minute knowledge-sharing session
- Running an English-language 1:1 discussion about your AI project
- Contributing to a team meeting in English
