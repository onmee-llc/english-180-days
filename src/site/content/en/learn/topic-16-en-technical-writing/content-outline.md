## Writing milestones by week

**Week 1 (Days 197–203):** Topic → Outline → Introduction → First 4 sections
**Week 2 (Days 204–210):** Complete draft → Review → Final edit → Publish

## Speaking session types

| Session | Recommended format |
|---------|-------------------|
| Session 1 | 5-minute pitch: "I'm writing an article about X because Y" — to one colleague |
| Session 2 | 10-minute knowledge share: present the article's main finding to the team |
| Session 3 | 15-minute 1:1 discussion: discuss a technical decision from your KPI 1 or 2 work in English |
| Session 4 | 5-minute reflection: "What I found difficult about writing in English and how I improved" |

## Learning objectives

By the end of Topic 16, you will be able to:
- Write a 600–1200 word technical article in English suitable for publication
- Structure a technical argument: problem → approach → outcome → recommendation
- Use AI grammar tools effectively without letting them replace your own voice
- Lead a 15-minute technical discussion in English with colleagues
- Publish and share your work on a professional platform
