## Why this topic

Behavioral and project-based interviews are as common as technical ones at senior level. STAR = Situation, Task, Action, Result — the universal structure international interviewers expect.

## Structure

This topic has 4 modules:

1. **Content Outline** — Core concepts sorted by importance
2. **Key Vocabulary** — Phrases and words you must internalize
3. **Practice Questions** — 5 questions to drill until automatic
4. **Weekly Goals** — Specific targets for Week 1 and Week 2

Use the sidebar navigation to move through each module.

Every single day, before starting this topic, speak your **self-introduction** (Topic 1) once out loud.
This is non-negotiable for all 180 days — it becomes the foundation of everything.
