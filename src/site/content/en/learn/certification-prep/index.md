## What this track is

This is not a daily shadowing track — it's a structured study companion for your KPI 3 certification. It runs in parallel with Topics 12–14 from May through September 2026.

**Target course:** [Generative AI with LLMs](https://www.coursera.org/learn/generative-ai-with-llms) — DeepLearning.AI × AWS × Coursera (~16 hours, 3 weeks at recommended pace)

**KPI 3 deadline:** End of Q4 2026 (December). Certificate must be earned and shared on LinkedIn.

## Four modules

1. **Study Plan & Schedule** — How to pace the course alongside KPI 1 and 2 work
2. **Core Concepts** — The key theoretical concepts from the course + vocabulary to discuss them in English
3. **Practice & Labs** — How to approach the hands-on labs and connect them to your real work
4. **Exam Prep & Certification** — Final review, badge sharing, LinkedIn update

## Why DeepLearning.AI

- Practical focus: the course is taught by practitioners at deeplearning.ai and AWS, not academics
- Coursera certificate widely recognized in the tech industry
- ~16 hours — completable in 3 weeks at 1 hour/day, 5 days/week
- Content directly complements your KPI 1 and KPI 2 work

## Running in parallel

While doing this track, you're simultaneously:
- Building your KPI 1 system (Topics 12–14 in this program)
- Writing your KPI 2 LLM Best Practices Guide
- Practicing English for tech talks (Topics 14–16)

The certification study reinforces the theory behind the practical work you're doing in KPI 1 and 2. As you encounter a concept in the course, apply it immediately to your real project.

## Daily practice reminder

This track does not have daily shadowing lessons. Continue your regular daily English practice (Topics 12–16 or review) in parallel with certification study.
