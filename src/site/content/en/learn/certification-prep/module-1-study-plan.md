## Course overview

**Course:** Generative AI with LLMs — [Coursera link](https://www.coursera.org/learn/generative-ai-with-llms)

**Duration:** ~16 hours total content. At 1 hour/day, 5 days/week: 3.2 weeks. Build in buffer → **target 5 weeks**.

**Modules in the course:**
1. Introduction to LLMs and the generative AI project lifecycle
2. LLM pre-training and scaling laws
3. Fine-tuning LLMs with instruction tuning
4. Parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) and LoRA
5. Reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF)
6. LLM-powered applications: RAG and LLM agents
7. Responsible AI and LLM deployment

## How to take notes effectively

1. **Watch first, then take notes** — Don't pause every 30 seconds. Watch a full section (10–15 min), then write key points from memory.
2. **Write notes in English** — Even if your instinct is to write in Vietnamese. The practice of writing in English reinforces the vocabulary.
3. **Connect to your real work** — After every module, write one sentence: "This concept applies to my KPI 1 project because…"
4. **Use the Coursera discussion forums** — Asking and answering questions in English is KPI 6 practice (speaking/writing in English).
