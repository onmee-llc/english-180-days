## How to add to LinkedIn

1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
2. Click "Add profile section" → "Licenses & Certifications"
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Generative AI with LLMs
   - **Issuing organization:** DeepLearning.AI
   - **Issue date:** Your completion month and year
   - **Credential URL:** Your Coursera certificate URL
4. Save
