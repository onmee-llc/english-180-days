## Session goal

Adjust to a corporate or large-company interview: more formal, more structured.

## Shadowing passage

> My name is Robert, and I bring over twelve years of backend engineering experience to this role, with a focused specialization in AI systems and distributed architecture over the past four years. In my most recent position, I led the design and implementation of a production AI pipeline built on large language models, including a custom prompt management system, streaming inference handling, and an automated evaluation framework. Prior to that, I architected the core transaction orchestration service at a fintech company, where I had responsibility for system reliability across a high-volume, compliance-sensitive workload. Across both environments — fast-moving AI product development and regulated financial infrastructure — I have consistently delivered clean, maintainable systems that the broader engineering organization can extend with confidence. I am now pursuing senior engineering roles at organizations deploying AI capabilities at scale, where I can contribute both technical depth and architectural leadership. I would be very interested to hear about the specific challenges your team is currently working on.

## Key phrases

| Phrase | IPA | Note |
|--------|-----|------|
| I bring X to this role | /aɪ brɪŋ ... tuː ðɪs roʊl/ | immediately frames the interview as 'what value I add' |
| compliance-sensitive workload | /kəmˈplaɪəns ˈsɛnsɪtɪv ˈwɜːrkloʊd/ | corporate precision — signals you understand regulated environments |
| architectural leadership | /ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəl ˈliːdərʃɪp/ | aims higher than 'I write good code' |

## 30-minute protocol

**Minutes 1–5** — Read the passage silently twice. Focus on meaning, not pronunciation.

**Minutes 6–15** — Shadow aloud with the text visible. 4 full passes. Match rhythm and stress.

**Minutes 16–22** — Shadow without the text. 3 passes. Accept mistakes — keep moving.

**Minutes 23–28** — Record yourself once. Play it back immediately. Note one phrase to improve.

**Minutes 29–30** — Write the phrase you struggled with. Say it 10 times slowly, then at full speed.

## Reflection

Does this version sound too stiff? Try saying it with slightly more warmth while keeping the precision.
