## Session goal

Answer 'Why are you looking?' with confidence and a growth narrative.

## Shadowing passage

> I've been at my current company for about three years, and it's been a strong experience — I've had the chance to architect meaningful systems and grow technically. But I've reached the point where I'm no longer learning at the pace I want to, and the work has become fairly incremental. I'm ready for a bigger challenge. What specifically motivated me to start looking is the kind of work I've been doing on the side — building AI-powered tools, integrating LLMs, thinking about agent architectures. I want to do that kind of work at the center of a product, not as a side experiment. I've also been intentional about finding international, distributed teams. I want to grow in that direction — working with engineers across time zones, doing things asynchronously by default, and being held to a higher communication standard. I think that's where both the best opportunities and the most growth are right now.

## 30-minute protocol

**Minutes 1–5** — Read the passage silently twice. Focus on meaning, not pronunciation.

**Minutes 6–15** — Shadow aloud with the text visible. 4 full passes. Match rhythm and stress.

**Minutes 16–22** — Shadow without the text. 3 passes. Accept mistakes — keep moving.

**Minutes 23–28** — Record yourself once. Play it back immediately. Note one phrase to improve.

**Minutes 29–30** — Write the phrase you struggled with. Say it 10 times slowly, then at full speed.

## Reflection

Do you sound like you're running FROM something or toward something? Aim for the second.
