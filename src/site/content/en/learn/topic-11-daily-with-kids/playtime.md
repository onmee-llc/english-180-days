## Context

During free play and activity time.

## Phrases

| English phrase | Vietnamese context |
|---|---|
| "What do you want to play?" | Con muốn chơi gì? |
| "Let's play together." | Chơi cùng cha nha |
| "Be careful, okay?" | Cẩn thận nhé |
| "Share with your brother/sister." | Nhường cho em/anh |
| "Five more minutes, then we stop." | Còn 5 phút nữa thôi |
| "Are you having fun?" | Chơi vui không? |
| "Can Daddy play too?" | Cha chơi cùng được không? |

## How to practice

1. Read each phrase out loud once.
2. Cover the English column. Look at the Vietnamese — say the English phrase immediately.
3. Repeat until all phrases in this group come out with zero hesitation.

## Drill

Pick the hardest 2–3 phrases from this group. Use them in real situations today, whether or not the moment is perfect.
