## Context

Highest repetition — learn these first.

## Phrases

| English phrase | Vietnamese context |
|---|---|
| "Time to wake up, buddy." | Dậy đi con |
| "Did you sleep well?" | Ngủ ngon không? |
| "Let's brush your teeth first." | Đánh răng trước đi |
| "What do you want for breakfast?" | Con muốn ăn gì sáng nay? |
| "Hurry up, we need to get ready." | Nhanh lên, chuẩn bị đi |
| "Put your clothes on." | Mặc quần áo vào |
| "Time to get ready for school." | Chuẩn bị đi học |

## How to practice

1. Read each phrase out loud once.
2. Cover the English column. Look at the Vietnamese — say the English phrase immediately.
3. Repeat until all phrases in this group come out with zero hesitation.

## Drill

Pick the hardest 2–3 phrases from this group. Use them in real situations today, whether or not the moment is perfect.
