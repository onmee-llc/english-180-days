## Context

Use every school day, twice.

## Phrases

| English phrase | Vietnamese context |
|---|---|
| "How was school today?" | Hôm nay con học thế nào? |
| "What did you learn today?" | Con học gì hôm nay? |
| "Did you play with your friends?" | Con có chơi với bạn không? |
| "Tell me something fun that happened." | Kể cha nghe chuyện vui đi |
| "Who did you sit next to today?" | Con ngồi cạnh ai? |
| "Did you eat all your lunch?" | Con ăn hết bữa trưa chưa? |
| "Did you miss me?" | Con có nhớ cha không? |

## How to practice

1. Read each phrase out loud once.
2. Cover the English column. Look at the Vietnamese — say the English phrase immediately.
3. Repeat until all phrases in this group come out with zero hesitation.

## Drill

Pick the hardest 2–3 phrases from this group. Use them in real situations today, whether or not the moment is perfect.
