## Context

Every evening — bath, pajamas, story, sleep.

## Phrases

| English phrase | Vietnamese context |
|---|---|
| "Time to take a bath." | Tới giờ tắm |
| "Put on your pajamas." | Mặc đồ ngủ vào |
| "Let's read a book before bed." | Đọc sách trước khi ngủ nhé |
| "Close your eyes, it's bedtime." | Nhắm mắt vào, ngủ rồi |
| "I love you." | Cha thương con |
| "Good night. Sweet dreams." | Ngủ ngon |
| "No screen time before bed." | Không có điện thoại trước khi ngủ |

## How to practice

1. Read each phrase out loud once.
2. Cover the English column. Look at the Vietnamese — say the English phrase immediately.
3. Repeat until all phrases in this group come out with zero hesitation.

## Drill

Pick the hardest 2–3 phrases from this group. Use them in real situations today, whether or not the moment is perfect.
