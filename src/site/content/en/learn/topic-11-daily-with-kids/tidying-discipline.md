## Context

After playtime, before transitions.

## Phrases

| English phrase | Vietnamese context |
|---|---|
| "Please put your toys away." | Dọn đồ chơi đi |
| "Everything goes back in the box." | Cất vào hộp đi |
| "We take care of our things." | Mình phải giữ gìn đồ đạc |
| "Let's do it together." | Làm cùng cha nha |
| "We don't do that, okay?" | Không được làm vậy |
| "Do you understand?" | Con hiểu không? |
| "Say sorry, please." | Nói xin lỗi đi |

## How to practice

1. Read each phrase out loud once.
2. Cover the English column. Look at the Vietnamese — say the English phrase immediately.
3. Repeat until all phrases in this group come out with zero hesitation.

## Drill

Pick the hardest 2–3 phrases from this group. Use them in real situations today, whether or not the moment is perfect.
