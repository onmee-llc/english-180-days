## How this track works

This is **not** a separate 30-minute session. Use these phrases naturally throughout the day.

- No study time required — just use the phrases in real situations.
- Each group maps to a daily routine context: morning, meals, bedtime, etc.
- The goal is **automatic production** — you say the phrase without thinking, just like in Vietnamese.

## Children ages

3 and 4 years old.

## Learning pace

| Week | Groups to focus on |
|------|---|
| 1–2 | Group 1 (Morning) + Group 3 (Mealtime) — highest daily repetition |
| 3–4 | Group 2 (School) + Group 4 (Tidying) |
| 5–6 | Group 5 (Help) + Group 6 (Playtime) |
| 7–8 | Group 7 (Bedtime) + Group 8 (Praise — begin using from Day 1) |
| 9+ | All groups in natural rotation — aim for zero hesitation |

## Important note

Group 8 (Encouragement & Praise) should be used **from Day 1**, every day, regardless of which other groups you're focusing on.
