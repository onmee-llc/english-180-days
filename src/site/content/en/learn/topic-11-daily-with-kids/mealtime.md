## Context

3 times a day — breakfast, lunch, dinner.

## Phrases

| English phrase | Vietnamese context |
|---|---|
| "It's time to eat." | Tới giờ ăn rồi |
| "Wash your hands before eating." | Rửa tay trước khi ăn |
| "Come sit down at the table." | Ngồi vào bàn ăn đi |
| "Do you want more rice?" | Con muốn thêm cơm không? |
| "Eat your vegetables first." | Ăn rau trước đi |
| "No playing while eating, okay?" | Không chơi trong khi ăn nhé |
| "Is it delicious?" | Con ăn ngon không? |
| "Finish your food and I'll give you a treat." | Ăn hết đi rồi cha thưởng |

## How to practice

1. Read each phrase out loud once.
2. Cover the English column. Look at the Vietnamese — say the English phrase immediately.
3. Repeat until all phrases in this group come out with zero hesitation.

## Drill

Pick the hardest 2–3 phrases from this group. Use them in real situations today, whether or not the moment is perfect.
