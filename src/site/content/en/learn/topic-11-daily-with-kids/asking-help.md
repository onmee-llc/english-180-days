## Context

Whenever you need the kids involved.

## Phrases

| English phrase | Vietnamese context |
|---|---|
| "Can you help Daddy with this?" | Con phụ cha cái này được không? |
| "Hold this for me, please." | Cầm cái này cho cha |
| "Can you bring me that?" | Lấy cái đó cho cha |
| "You're such a good helper!" | Con giỏi quá, phụ cha giỏi |
| "Thank you, that was very helpful." | Cảm ơn con nhiều lắm |
| "I need your help for a moment." | Cha cần con giúp một chút |

## How to practice

1. Read each phrase out loud once.
2. Cover the English column. Look at the Vietnamese — say the English phrase immediately.
3. Repeat until all phrases in this group come out with zero hesitation.

## Drill

Pick the hardest 2–3 phrases from this group. Use them in real situations today, whether or not the moment is perfect.
