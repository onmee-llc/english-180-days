## Context

Use throughout every day — from Day 1, all 180 days.

## Phrases

| English phrase | Vietnamese context |
|---|---|
| "Good job! / Well done!" | Con giỏi lắm |
| "I'm proud of you." | Cha tự hào về con |
| "You did it!" | Con làm được rồi! |
| "Keep trying, you can do it." | Cố lên, con làm được |
| "That's amazing!" | Tuyệt vời quá! |
| "You're so smart." | Con thông minh lắm |
| "I love how you did that." | Cha thích cách con làm |
| "You're getting better every day." | Con đang tiến bộ lắm |

## How to practice

1. Read each phrase out loud once.
2. Cover the English column. Look at the Vietnamese — say the English phrase immediately.
3. Repeat until all phrases in this group come out with zero hesitation.

## Drill

Pick the hardest 2–3 phrases from this group. Use them in real situations today, whether or not the moment is perfect.
