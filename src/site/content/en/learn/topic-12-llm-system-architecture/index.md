## Why this topic

Describing a system you built is one of the hardest things to do in a second language. You need to explain data flows, justify decisions, and answer follow-up questions — all while sounding like a Principal Engineer, not a junior dev reading from notes.

This topic gives you the exact vocabulary and sentence patterns for every layer of LLM system design.

## Structure

This topic has 4 modules:

1. **Content Outline** — Core concepts sorted by importance
2. **Key Vocabulary** — Phrases and words you must internalize
3. **Practice Questions** — 5 questions to drill until automatic
4. **Weekly Goals** — Specific targets for Week 1 and Week 2

Use the sidebar navigation to move through each module.

## KPI 1 connection

Every lesson in this topic is directly tied to the architecture work you are doing in KPI 1. As you build, use these phrases in your architecture doc, your README, and your tech talk slides.
