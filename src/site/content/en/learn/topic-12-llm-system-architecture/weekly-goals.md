## Week 1 Goal

Describe the complete data flow of your KPI 1 system aloud — without notes — in under 4 minutes. Cover every layer: client, API gateway, your service, LLM API, post-processing, response delivery.

**How to verify:** Record yourself. Can a new engineer understand the full flow from your description alone? If yes, you've hit the goal.

## Week 2 Goal

Answer all 5 practice questions without preparation. For Question 1 (system walkthrough), aim for 4 minutes of clean, structured explanation.

**How to verify:** Do a full dry run — start the recording, ask yourself the question, answer as if it's a real system design interview.

## KPI 1 action

While doing this topic, update your KPI 1 architecture document with the vocabulary you're learning. Use the English terms in your diagrams and ADRs. This is the fastest way to internalize them — real writing beats flashcards.

## If you need more time

If the goals don't feel comfortable after 2 weeks, extend by 3–5 days before moving on.
**Fluency over schedule.**
