## Session goal

Practice the structure of an efficient help request that respects the expert's time.

## Shadowing passage

> I need to ask for help with something. I have been working on an issue for about two hours and I am stuck. Here is the context: I am trying to configure a Kubernetes liveness probe for a service that takes about 45 seconds to start up correctly. The problem is that Kubernetes is killing the pod before it finishes initializing because the liveness probe starts too early. I have tried increasing the initial delay on the liveness probe to 60 seconds, but the pod is still being restarted. I looked at the Kubernetes docs and I believe I am using the correct field, but I must be missing something. Here is the specific thing I need: could you take a look at this YAML configuration and see if you spot what I am getting wrong? I will paste it in the thread. I think it should take about five minutes — if it is more complex than that, just let me know and I will schedule time properly rather than troubleshoot async. I appreciate it.

## Key phrases

| Phrase | IPA | Note |
|--------|-----|------|
| I am stuck | /aɪ æm stʌk/ | honest, direct — not a sign of weakness, a signal that help is needed |
| paste it in the thread | /peɪst ɪt ɪn ðə θrɛd/ | keep the conversation organized in async tools |
| schedule time properly | /ˈʃɛdjuːl taɪm ˈprɒpərli/ | respect that async debugging is not always the right tool |

## 30-minute protocol

**Minutes 1–5** — Read the passage silently twice. Focus on meaning, not pronunciation.

**Minutes 6–15** — Shadow aloud with the text visible. 4 full passes. Match rhythm and stress.

**Minutes 16–22** — Shadow without the text. 3 passes. Accept mistakes — keep moving.

**Minutes 23–28** — Record yourself once. Play it back immediately. Note one phrase to improve.

**Minutes 29–30** — Write the phrase you struggled with. Say it 10 times slowly, then at full speed.

## Reflection

Does your help request give enough context that the expert doesn't need to ask follow-ups first?
