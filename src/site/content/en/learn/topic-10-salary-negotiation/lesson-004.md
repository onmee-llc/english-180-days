## Session goal

Do not accept the first 'no' — learn to expand the negotiation scope.

## Shadowing passage

> They say: 'We really do not have room to move on the base. Five thousand is the budget for this role.' Here are four things you can respond with, in order of preference. One: 'I understand. Could we look at an earlier performance review — at six months instead of twelve? If the base at that point reflects strong performance, that addresses my concern.' Two: 'Is there flexibility on the equity component? Even a small grant would help close the gap.' Three: 'Would the company consider a remote work equipment stipend or a professional development budget? Even three to five hundred dollars a year would be meaningful to me.' Four: 'I hear you. Given the role and the team, I am willing to accept the offer at five thousand. I do want to put on record that I believe the market rate for this specialization is higher, and I hope we can revisit this at the twelve-month mark.' Notice that each response keeps the conversation going. The goal is not to be difficult — it is to leave no value on the table without realizing it. Only accept 'no' as final when you have exhausted all vectors.

## Key phrases

| Phrase | IPA | Note |
|--------|-----|------|
| leave no value on the table | /liːv nəʊ ˈvæljuː ɒn ðə ˈteɪbəl/ | negotiation idiom — don't give up something you could have asked for |
| put on record | /pʊt ɒn ˈrɛkərd/ | make it formally known, even if not immediately actioned |
| exhausted all vectors | /ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd ɔːl ˈvɛktərz/ | tried every possible angle before accepting the final answer |

## 30-minute protocol

**Minutes 1–5** — Read the passage silently twice. Focus on meaning, not pronunciation.

**Minutes 6–15** — Shadow aloud with the text visible. 4 full passes. Match rhythm and stress.

**Minutes 16–22** — Shadow without the text. 3 passes. Accept mistakes — keep moving.

**Minutes 23–28** — Record yourself once. Play it back immediately. Note one phrase to improve.

**Minutes 29–30** — Write the phrase you struggled with. Say it 10 times slowly, then at full speed.
