## How to use this module

For each phrase or term:
1. Say it out loud 3 times.
2. Use it in a sentence about your own situation.
3. Practice using it naturally until it feels confident, not awkward.

**Goal:** Zero hesitation when money is discussed in an interview.

## Daily drill

Pick 3–5 phrases from this list each day. Say each one out loud in a complete sentence before moving on.

---

## Compensation Terms

| Term / Phrase | Usage Example |
|---------------|---------------|
| base salary | "The base salary for this role is five thousand per month." |
| total compensation | "When I evaluate offers, I look at total compensation, not just base." |
| equity / stock options | "Does the role include equity? What's the vesting schedule?" |
| benefits package | "The benefits package includes health insurance, dental, and a learning budget." |
| remote work allowance | "Is there a remote work allowance for equipment and internet?" |
| performance review | "When is the first performance review and salary adjustment?" |
| signing bonus | "Would a signing bonus be an option to bridge the gap?" |
| vesting schedule | "The equity vests over four years with a one-year cliff." |

## Negotiation Phrases

| Phrase | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| "Based on my experience and the scope of this role..." | Anchoring your expectation on value |
| "I'm targeting between X and Y per month." | Stating your range |
| "I'm open to discussing the full package." | Inviting a broader conversation |
| "Is there flexibility on the base salary?" | Direct but professional ask |
| "I was hoping we could get to X." | Soft negotiation language |
| "What does the compensation structure look like?" | Turning the question back |
| "Based on market research for this role..." | Using data to support your ask |

## Responding to Offers

| Phrase | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| "Thank you for the offer — I'm genuinely interested." | Opening your response positively |
| "Could I have a couple of days to review?" | Buying time professionally |
| "The offer is close to what I had in mind, but..." | Soft lead-in to negotiation |
| "Is there room to move on the base?" | Direct negotiation |
| "Could we revisit this at the six-month mark?" | When they can't move on salary now |

## When They Push Back

| Phrase | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| "I understand. Are there other aspects of the package that might be adjustable?" | Pivoting to non-salary items |
| "Would an earlier performance review be possible?" | Alternative to higher base |
| "I'm excited about this role — I just want to make sure we find something that works for both sides." | Keeping it collaborative |
| "What would it take to reach X within the first year?" | Long-term framing |
